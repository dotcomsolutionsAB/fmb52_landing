About Us

Every mumin cherishes the barakat from Moula’s thaal, a gift that transcends the physical, nourishing both body and soul. What began as a dream—receiving daily meals blessed by our beloved Moula TUS—has become a cherished reality, infusing barakat into the lives and homes of thousands of mumineen worldwide.

In the auspicious year of 1432H, coinciding with the 100th Milaad Mubarak of Moulana al Hayy al Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin RA, and with the sacred permission of Moulana, his Mansoos, Syedna Aali Qadar Mufaddal Saifuddin TUS, launched Faiz ul Mawaid il Burhaniyah. This initiative, rooted in the niyyat of Moulana’s tulul umr, has become a lifeline for mumineen, providing healthy, hygienic, and halaal meals directly to their doorsteps, unifying all under the banner of Moula’s munificence.

Faiz ul Mawaid il Burhaniyah now touches nearly 100,000 homes globally, enriching lives with the daily thaali that brings health, relief, and unity. It symbolizes the essence of Moula’s generosity, bringing mumineen closer to their beloved SyednaTUS.
